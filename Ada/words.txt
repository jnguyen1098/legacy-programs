Aesop was one of the great Greek writers.
He is best known for his fables, stories that have a moral.
They teach us something about how we should live our lives.
Aesop wrote thousands of these stories. Here are a few.
The Wolf in the Clothing of the Sheep
Once upon a time, a Wolf wanted to get more food. He went into the
barn and stole the skin of a sheep. But the farmer killed him for doing so.
